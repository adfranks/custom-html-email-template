The summer is a wonderful time of year to go for a hike. Take a deep breath of fresh air and go hiking this summer. Stretch those legs and get outside with heart beating and a smile across your face. Check out some new trip reports to whet your appetite.

Zion National Park of Southern Utah

You can't get much better than hiking throughout Zion National Park. This one is a beauty! Orange and cream rock formations rise out of the ground and tower over you as you thread your way through this land. Indulge in what nature has to offer this summer.

TAKE ACTION TO...

Minimize your carbon footprint by carpooling

Find somebody who is planning on going for a hike as well! Join a local hiking group that shares rides to the trailhead. This way you do less damage to the natural beauty you plan to enjoy.

Stay healthy

Chronic diseases are shortening people's lives by many years. Often these diseases can be avoided by simply choosing a healhty diet and a bit of regular exercise. A great way to stick to it is to choose healthy foods you actually like and exercise that exerts yourself a bit and you truly enjoy. Extend your life.

Feel Good

One of the best ways to feel good is to get up and exercise. While it may take a little motivation to get out of that comfy chair, once you're getting some cardio exercise, you'll thank yourself for it.
